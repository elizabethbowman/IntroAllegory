Section 1 — Key Terms

Before reading Animal Farm, you need three vocabulary words in your toolkit. Tap each card to flip it and reveal the definition.

Fable
Tap to reveal →
A short story — often with animal characters — that teaches a moral lesson. Think Aesop: "The Tortoise and the Hare."
Satire
Tap to reveal →
Using humor, irony, or exaggeration to expose and criticize a real-world flaw or problem — hoping society will notice and change.
Allegory
Tap to reveal →
A story where characters, events, and settings are symbols representing deeper real-world ideas — the surface story has a hidden meaning.
